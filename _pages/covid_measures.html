COVID-19 MEASURES


Dear Guests,

To ensure that you have a safe and worry-free stay at Fawzy Hotel, we have introduced the following COVID-19 protection measures:

  1. Sanitizers and hand washing stations have been installed in key locations.Our guests are encouraged to always use face mask as directed by the authorities.
  2. Hotel staff are equipped with PPE (personal protective equipment)
  3. Hotel premises (indoors and outdoors) are regularly disinfected
  4. The hotel performs temperature checks on staff and guests on regular basis
  5. If you have symptoms of COVID-19 please self-isolate and or report/call appropriate authorities. Please do not visit our hotel as mandated by the government to ensure a reduced spread of the disease

Thank you for your understanding amidst this special circumstance.
We hope to welcome you as we create a healthy and safe environment for you to stay.

Kind Regards,
Fawzy Hotel Team
